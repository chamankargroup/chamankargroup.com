At Chamankar Group, we believe architecture should breathe. Our designs embrace natural forms and organic materials, creating spaces that feel alive and connected to their environment.

Studio Image

Our Story

Founded in 1999 by visionary architect Rajesh Chamankar, our studio began with a simple belief: architecture should enhance life, not dominate it.

Over 25 years, we've grown from a small Mumbai practice into one of India's most respected architecture firms, yet our core philosophy remains unchanged. Every project, whether a family home or a towering commercial complex, begins with understanding how people will live, work, and thrive within our spaces.

Today, with over 150 completed projects and numerous industry accolades, we continue to push boundaries while staying true to our organic design philosophy.
